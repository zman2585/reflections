When would you want to use a remote repository rather than keeping all your work local?
--- when working with a team of people who all need access to the files

Why might you want to always pull changes manually rather than having Git automatically stay up-to-date with your remote repository?
--- to keep the remote branch from becoming corrupted or error filed

Describe the differences between forks, clones, and branches. When would you use one instead of another?

---forks are clones created on github, they link back and give credit to the original creators
--- clones are local copies of a repo
--- branches are different paths that your code takes
--- use forks on github, cloning to have local copies and branches to test new features before going live
